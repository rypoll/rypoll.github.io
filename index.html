About▼

  • The AI Digestive is a newsletter digest that summarizes AI news across various email newsletters using OpenAI.
  • Each news item's related hyperlink is assigned a popularity score based on the number of backlinks the link has.
  • A backlink serves as a measure of the popularity of the link. A backlink is the number of time that link has been linked to across the internet.


Overall, this website serves as a central repository to find the latest advancements in AI in a quick and simple way, sorted by popularity across desired time-frames.
